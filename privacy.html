This app (“Poker GTO Trainer: Preflop”, the “App”) is a pre-flop poker decision trainer. We care about your privacy and collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what is and isn’t collected.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not require an account, sign-up, or login.
  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or contacts.
  • We do not show ads and do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

Data stored on your device

Your training progress, statistics, settings, and review history are stored locally on your device (browser localStorage). This data stays on your device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed if you uninstall the App or clear its data.

Purchases (Pro subscription / one-time unlock)

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing and managed through RevenueCat, our subscription infrastructure provider. To process and restore purchases, RevenueCat receives a randomly generated anonymous app user ID, your purchase/subscription status, and basic device/store information. We never receive your Google account password or full payment card details.

  • Google Play Privacy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat Privacy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Children

The App involves poker strategy and is intended for adults. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing the App’s data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App. To manage or cancel a subscription, use the Google Play Store subscription settings.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email tangzy09@gmail.com.
